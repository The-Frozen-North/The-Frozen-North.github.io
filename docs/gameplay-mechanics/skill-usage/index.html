Skill Usage

Bluff

  • Used to gain access to restricted areas.
  • Used to complete a bounty instantly. Bounties completed in this fashion only reward half the XP.

Persuade

  • Used in most services to get a discount.
  • Used to persuade more than one henchman or follower to join your party.
  • Tip: Always try the persuade option before paying full price - who knows, you might get a lucky roll and save yourself some coin.

Appraise

  • Has a direct influence on merchant prices.

Disable Trap

  • Disabling or recovering a trap now rewards XP, based on the difficulty of the trap.

Pick pocket

  • Most NPCs and humanoids can be pick pocketed for additional loot.
  • Loot you can get from pick pocket do not drop on death, so it may be wise to pick pocket an enemy before killing them.
  • If you have angered some townsfolk or guards by pick pocketing, exiting the area should reset their hostility.
  • Players are immune to pick pocket.

Ride

  • 30% spell failure while mounted. Spell failure reduced by 10% for every 5 ranks of the Ride skill.
  • Mount speed is influenced by this skill.
